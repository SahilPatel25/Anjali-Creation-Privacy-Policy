Privacy Policy

Effective Date: 29-05-2024

Anjali Creation ("we", "our", or "us") operates the Anjali Creation mobile application (the "App"). This Privacy Policy informs you of our policies regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of personal information we receive from users of the App.

Permissions

  • Internet Access: The App requires access to the internet to load product data and share products via WhatsApp.
  • Network State Access: The App requires access to network state to ensure it can connect to the internet.
  • Read Media Images: The App requires permission to read media images to allow users to select images from their gallery.
  • Write External Storage: The App requires permission to write to external storage to save selected images.

Information Collection and Use

1. Personal Information:

Phone Number: When you log in, we ask for your phone number. This is used to create and secure your account.

Profile Information: After logging in, you'll create a profile. This may include your name, profile picture, and other optional details.

2. Usage Information:

Trends Viewed: We track the latest fashion trends you look at. This helps us show you more of what you like.

Sharing Activity: If you share trends with colleagues, we record this to improve your sharing experience.

3. Device Information:

Device Details: We collect information about the device you use, such as the type of device, operating system, and version. This helps us make sure the app works well on your device.

4. Log Data:

Log Files: We automatically collect some data when you use the app. This includes your IP address, app version, and the time you spend on different sections of the app. This helps us understand how you use the app and improve it.
